Welcome to #hoodie!

Welcome to our IRC channel on irc.freenode.net/#hoodie>. We're a small team and can't always be here, but we'd still like to make it easy to get in touch with us. We're usually here on weekdays between 14:00 CET and 18:00 CET.

We also have an FAQ for the most common questions.

If you have a specific issue or problem, we'd also like to point you to our github issue pages:

  • Hoodie Command Line Interface
  • Hoodie.js library
  • General topics and discussions

If you'd just like to talk about general things, you can ping us on Twitter (@hoodiehq), or check out our further contact details, and then we'll go from there. Either way, we look forward to hearing from you!

Thanks for dropping by!
